Our Idea


Guardian will aims to provide a platform where can connect with each other and stay safe when they go out. The app features a social network that allows users to connect with each other and share their experiences, as well as tools for staying safe in various situations. These might include features like real-time location sharing, emergency alerts, and a directory of resources for women in need of assistance. The app will also provide educational resources and support services, such as self-defense classes, counseling services, and mentorship programs. The goal of the app is to empower women without mothers by providing them with the resources they need to stay safe and build a supportive community.
